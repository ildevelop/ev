Official statistics show that Facebook has around 968 million daily users (correct as of July 2015). Very few other sites on the web can compete with such impressive figures. In fact, only Google, YouTube, Yahoo and Amazon even come close.

admin 7 months לפני 0

Perhaps that’s why more and more people are turning to Facebook to market their products and services. With so many potential consumers just waiting to be discovered on there, this social media site is a veritable gold mine waiting to be mined.

Why Facebook is a Great Place to Market Your Business

While much of Facebook’s appeal is down to the number of visitors using the site, there are other good reasons to use it for online marketing, too.

In recent years, Facebook has developed its service to be more business-friendly. As a result, having a Facebook business page is now a necessity if you trade online. The investment in Facebook advertising has also enticed more retailers to use the platform to advertise on. With such a vast array of people using the service, it’s a great place to test out some of your most successful advertising techniques.

Getting started on Facebook couldn’t be easier. It takes just moments to create an account, and not too much longer to optimize one for success. Of course, regular communication with customers will still be required, but this is true for every social media marketing- or digital marketing campaign.

Developing a Successful Facebook Marketing Strategy

Facebook’s biggest advantage can also be it’s disadvantage. The amount of people using the platform daily means there’s much competition for attention. Games, news, pictures, status updates, ads, videos and more all compete against your business for the recognition of others. That means, Facebook marketing strategies really ought to be well thought out if they’re to be successful.

Any strategy needs to focus on understanding potential customers and what they desire so that a solution to their problems can be offered. This strategy should also focus on being as engaging as possible. Engaging content is likely to get liked and shared, which is key if you’re set on improving customer engagement levels.

Facebook Marketing Tips

If you want to be a success on Facebook, there are several bits of advice worth taking note of.

First, treat Facebook marketing like any digital marketing campaign. Go in with a strategy. Know who your campaign is aimed at, and make sure you’ve done enough research to know what the market needs.

Second, try to capture your audience’s attention. Whether you’re using Facebook ads or writing on your Facebook business page, this applies. Make your content short, snappy and to the point. Solve problems, meet needs, and entertain followers.

This is usually enough to get the ball rolling. Of course, if you want further information, you can always ask the experts at your favorite online marketing agency to help you out.
